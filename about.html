About Itende Style App
Itende Style App it's a mobile application that explains how to set Itende style on different types of music keyboards that feature on the local style played in south africa called Itende Style.
It feature provides you recommended nearest stores where you can buy your desired keyboard.

What is Itende Style ?
it's a local Gospel music style played mainly using Fretless Bs or Fingerd Bs tone dual with Brightness Tone almost always.Started to be used on TENT revivals util it bridged into churches and music industry.

What are Famous Gospel singers of Itende Style ?

  • Hlengiwe Mhlaba
  • Mxolisi Mbethe
  • Thobekile
